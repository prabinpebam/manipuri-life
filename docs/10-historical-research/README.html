Domain 10

Historical Research

The Knowledge Bible - the single source of truth about life in historical Manipur, from which every mechanic, character, and asset is derived.

TL;DR

Research is organised into ten thematic branches plus two meta-documents (method and database schema). Nothing enters the game unless it can be traced to a rated entry here. This is where the project earns the word "accurate."

Purpose

First, the era

How deep each branch below goes depends on when the game is set - a decision still open between five fifty-year windows. See Era & Canonical Year for the framework and per-era notes.

Owns
The Knowledge Bible, the structured fact database, the source list, and the confidence model.
Feeds
Game Design (mechanics), Narrative (characters & folklore), Art & Audio (reconstruction).
Priority
P0 - the spine of the whole project.

The Knowledge Bible at a glance

What we already know (seed facts)

From initial research, a commoner's world in these centuries looked roughly like this. Each claim below becomes a rated entry in the fact database.

ThemeSeed factConfidence
SettlementsBamboo-timber-thatch houses raised off damp ground; pigs, poultry, cattle kept nearby.High
EconomyRice farming dominates; women weave clothing, manage finances, and run markets (roots of Ima Keithel).High
FoodRice, river fish, fermented fish (ngari), bamboo shoots, yams, herbs; milder spicing than mainland India.High
ClothingMen: cotton wrap (pheijom) + shawl. Women: phanek + innaphi. Natural dyes common.High
GovernmentLallup: adult men periodically owe labour/military service to the state instead of only cash.High
ReligionShift under Garib Niwaz (Pamheiba) toward Vaishnavism; older Meitei beliefs persist alongside.Medium
RecreationSagol Kangjei (precursor to polo), wrestling, boat races, music, festivals, storytelling.Medium
