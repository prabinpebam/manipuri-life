Domain 70

Cultural Integrity

The conscience of the project - making sure we represent a living culture with accuracy, respect, and the community's involvement.

TL;DR

This domain brings historians, archaeologists, linguists, craftspeople and elders in from the start, sets sensitivity guidelines, plans community engagement and localization, and keeps the game accessible. It is P0 because getting this wrong undermines everything else.

Purpose

Owns
Advisory board, cultural-sensitivity guidelines, community engagement, localization, and accessibility.
Governs
All content-producing domains - it has veto over misrepresentation.
Priority
P0

Advisors are collaborators, not fact-checkers

Bring the advisory circle in at the beginning and let them challenge assumptions early - before inaccuracies become part of the game's foundation.
