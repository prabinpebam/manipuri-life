Production & Roadmap

How the project is actually made: milestones, people, pipeline, and the risks we are steering around.

TL;DR

Production sequences the work, defines roles, and keeps a live risk register. The headline risk is "Fantasy Manipur" - a world that looks authentic but behaves generically - which is why research and cultural integrity are P0.

P1

Indicative phasing

  1. Phase A - Foundation & Research

    Fix the canonical year; stand up the Knowledge Bible and fact database.

  2. Phase B - Design vertical slice

    Prove the core loop (farming + market + one festival + Lallup) in one village.

  3. Phase C - Content & systems build

    Scale NPCs, quests, crafts, seasons and the emotional arc.

  4. Phase D - Polish, review & launch

    Advisory sign-off, localization, accessibility, and release.
