Domain 00

Project Foundation

The decisions everything else inherits: what we are making, for whom, in exactly which year, and how we will know it worked.

TL;DR

Foundation locks the vision (a "playable ethnography" of historical Manipur), the pillars, the scope boundaries, the single canonical year, the success metrics, and a shared glossary. Until these are fixed, downstream domains cannot converge.

Purpose

Owns
Vision, design pillars, scope, the canonical year, success metrics, and project vocabulary.
Feeds
Every other domain — especially Game Design, Historical Research, and Production.
Priority
P0 — blocks all downstream work.

The one decision that unblocks the rest

Pick a single year

"1715 vs 1728 vs 1742" are three different worlds — different religion, wars, festivals, script, crops, and roads. The Canonical Year Decision document resolves this and cascades certainty into every other page.
